Age and Wealth Distribution among Billionaires in Top 5 States in US

The scatter plot shows the age versus the networth of the billionaires in the top 5 state with the most number of billionaires.In the previous scatter plot the age and Wealth is prioritized by giving x and y encoding and the states were color encoded. The Scatter plot if not effective in communicating the results as the data points are overlapped and densly distributed below 20 Billion and the color schemes are not effective for people with color blindness. The updated plot is made more effective by removing color encoding making it accessible for the people with colorblindness and adding an interaction. Selection Histogram is used as interaction feature with fixed to the Age variable(x-axis) in the scatter plot and adapted to the bar chart with States along the y- axis to make it readable and prioritizing the count by giving positional x encoding below. The user can now select the age interval and the number people distributed in those interval with respect to the state are shown in the bar graph. Interaction has increased the effectiveness and accessiblity of the plot with accurate values and easily comparable between the states.
